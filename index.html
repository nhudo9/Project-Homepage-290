3 Day Weekend

Project Overview

An automated, hour-by-hour trip planner that creates personalized, hyper-curated three-day weekend itineraries highlighting the best things to do in a city. Travelers set their own traveler/trip preferences and the application generates a fully custom itinerary. Travelers would have the ability to modify the itinerary by locking recommendations or swiping them away to receive a new suggestion. Travelers can also update their trip preferences to update all unlocked recommendations.
